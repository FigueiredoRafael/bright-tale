Legend Card Behavior

  • Active state: Color-tinted background, colored top bar, checkmark in toggle box, full opacity
  • Toggled off: Faded to ~45% opacity, top bar dimmed, empty toggle box, line hidden from chart
  • Hover: Border brightens slightly (both states)
  • Content: Series label, current period value (big number), delta vs previous period
  • Click: Toggles chart line visibility — Recharts hide prop on the corresponding <Line>
  • Top color bar: 3px gradient matching the series line color — visual anchor connecting card to chart line
