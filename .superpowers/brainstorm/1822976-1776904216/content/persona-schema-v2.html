How Soul reaches the Blog Agent
Soul fields are injected into the Blog Agent's system prompt alongside voice. The agent is told: "This author finds comedy in contradiction. Use dry humor sparingly — never forced. Drop a recurring joke when the evidence gives you an opening. Express strong opinions as conclusions, not rants."
Why this fixes EEAT: Google's Experience signal isn't just "did you do this thing?" — it's "does this author have a consistent worldview that shapes how they interpret evidence?" Soul is what makes the same statistics produce a different article depending on who's writing. That's not replicable by generic AI.
