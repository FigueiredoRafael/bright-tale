B. Brand Dark + Polish

Same surface scale but with: brand logo badge, active indicator bar on sidebar, subtle brand glow on cards, stat cards with icons, richer empty state with radial glow. More visual personality.

Key fixes in both options:

  • Sidebar full-height — stretches to bottom of viewport (currently cuts off)
  • Background aligned with brand — #0A1017 instead of #050A0D (less harsh black)
  • Active nav = brand teal — instead of orange accent (which is for CTAs)
  • Card elevation — #141E2A cards on #0A1017 base (visible contrast)
  • Sidebar surface — #0F1620 (slightly elevated from base)
