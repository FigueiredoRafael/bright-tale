# Target Users

## Primary Persona

**Content creators who don't have time to produce at scale.**

- Bloggers who want to publish more frequently
- YouTubers who need scripts and thumbnails
- Digital entrepreneurs using content for marketing
- Agencies producing content for clients

## Key Traits

- May be **non-technical** — UX must be minimal clicks
- Want **fast results** — topic → published content
- Value **quality** — don't want generic AI content
- Need **multi-format** — one topic becomes blog + video + shorts
